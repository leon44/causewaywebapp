Holy Island Causeway Tide Times

All times are displayed in UK (London) time zone, regardless of your device's location settings.

Indicated times are for driving across the causeway by car, which takes around 10 minutes.

Times are based on official data from Northumberland County Council. Always exercise caution when crossing the causeway.

This app provides safe crossing times based on tide predictions only. Weather conditions including high winds, heavy rain, fog, or storms can significantly affect the safety of crossing even during published safe times. Never attempt to cross if conditions appear dangerous, regardless of tide times. Always check current weather forecasts and observe local conditions before crossing.

In an emergency, call 999 immediately.
