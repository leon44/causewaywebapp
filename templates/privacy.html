Privacy Policy

Holy Island Causeway App & Website

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Holy Island Causeway app ("the App") handles information. The App is designed to provide tide time information and safe crossing windows for the Holy Island of Lindisfarne causeway in Northumberland, England.

The short version: we collect no personal data, store nothing about you, and have no way to identify you. Your use of the App is entirely private.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact details
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any other information that could be used to identify you

3. Data Storage

The App does not store any user data on our servers because we do not operate any servers. All tide time data used by the App is either bundled within the App itself or retrieved from publicly available tide data sources. No personal information is involved in this process.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs that collect personal data are included in the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be made available within the App and on the App Store listing. The date at the top of this policy will reflect when it was last revised. Since we collect no personal data, any changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, you can contact us via the App Store support link on the App's product page.

This privacy policy applies to the Holy Island Causeway iOS application and website.
